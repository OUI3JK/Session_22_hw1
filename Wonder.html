7 WONDERS OF THE WORLD

  • The Great Pyramid of Giza (Egypt): The oldest and largest pyramid in Giza, a remarkable tomb for Pharaoh Khufu.
  • The Hanging Gardens of Babylon (Iraq): An architectural wonder, described as a terraced garden with exotic plants, its existence remains debated.
  • The Statue of Zeus at Olympia (Greece): A gigantic ivory and gold statue of the Greek god Zeus, created by Phidias.
  • The Temple of Artemis at Ephesus (Turkey): A massive and ornate temple dedicated to the Greek goddess Artemis.
  • The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus (Turkey): A magnificent tomb for Mausolus and his wife Artemisia, giving us the word "mausoleum."
  • The Colossus of Rhodes (Greece): A bronze statue of the sun god Helios, once one of the tallest statues of the ancient world.
